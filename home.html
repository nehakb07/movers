About Us

Welcome to Movers(India) Pvt. Ltd.

In the year 1979, when the term globalization was an unheard-of phrase, a group of technocrats with a wealth of experience and expertise set up a company called Movers (India). Over time, this company made the world sit up and take notice. Right from its inception, Movers India has always believed that nothing can stop an idea whose time has come.

Ideas are the lifeline of any business, especially in an intensely competitive environment where only the fittest and most imaginative can flourish. While the Indian economy has undergone significant changes over the past decades, Movers India has adapted to these changes to stay in tune with market needs and aspirations.
